TOP FIVE RESTAURANTS IN FINLAND

Finland is very popular for its sustainability and happiness rankings, but have you ever thought about the culture and dining experiences you can have when enjoying your holidays?

We are here to help you with the selection of the top best restaurants in the capital region

  1. ABC Restaurant :

    2. This is one of the biggest restaurant in Helsinki Region and is one of the most popular one in the Nordics. It is very rare to find a table during the weekends.

  2. PQR Restaurant :

    3. If you have a low budget but want to enjoy the best delicacies in Finland, then we highly suggest you to get your boots on and go to PQR Restaurant. It is nearly 5 Kms from the centre.

  3. XYZ Restaurant :

  4. EFG Restaurant :

    5. A Restaurant for the new generation and has everything for different taste buds in their menu. Its one of the cheapest and you can expect it to be highly crowded during the evening.

    6. Ate too much?, now its time to drink. MNP Restaurant and Bar has a wide range of alcohol for you to get high and enjoy with your friends. Make sure to book your entry before-hand.
