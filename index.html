03 — about

The short version

I came to software development as a deliberate career change - which means I am here because I chose this field with open eyes, not because I drifted into it.

I am entering the industry at a moment when AI is reshaping what's expected of junior developers. My answer to that is simple: AI can generate code, so I practice the part that still gets hired - judgement. Understanding why a structure is right, what breaks when requirements change, and whether I could defend a decision in code review.

Outside the code: I study full-time, work part-time, and have learned to raise problems early instead of letting them grow - across languages and cultures.
